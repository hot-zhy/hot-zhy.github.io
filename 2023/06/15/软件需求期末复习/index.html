Different Requirement levels

What is a requirements

In user view

A condition or capability needed by a user to solve a problem or achieve an objective

in developer view

A condition or capability that must be met or possessed by a system or system component to satisfy a contract , standard, specification, or other formally imposed document

levels of requirements

business requirements

Business requirements represent high-level objectives of the organization or customer who requests the system

user requirements

  • user requirements describe user goals or tasks that users must be able to perform with the product
  • ways of representation:
    1. use cases
    2. scenario descriptions
    3. event-response tables

functional requirements

  • functional requirements specify the software functionality that the developers must build into the product to enable users to accomplish their tasks,thereby satisfying the business requirements

system requirements

system requirements describe the top-level requirements for a product that contains multiple- that is , a system

business rules

  • business rules includes corporate policies , govenment regulations , industry standard , accounting practices , and computional algotithms
  • business rules exit outside the boundaries of any specific software systems
  • 业务规则本身不是软件需求
  • 它们经常决定系统为了切合相关规则而必须包含哪些功能

non-functional requirements

non-functional requirements includes performance goals amd descriptions of quality attributes

  • non-functinoal requirements includes performance goals and descriptions of quality attributes
  • quality attributes describe the product’s characristics in various dimensions that are important either to users or developers

software requirements specification (SRS)

  • describes as fully as necessary the expected behavior of the software system
  • used in developments , testing , quality assurance, project management, and related project functions
  • contains functinal and non-functional requirements

feature

  • a set of logically related functional requirements that provides a capability to the user and enables the satisfaction of a business objective

what requirements are not

  • design detail
  • implementation detail
  • project plan information
  • testing information
